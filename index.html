Performed Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulation and hydrodynamic analysis of the DARPA SUBOFF submarine geometry using ANSYS Fluent. The project focused on turbulence modelling, mesh optimization, drag prediction, and validation against experimental data to evaluate underwater performance.

Supersonic Missile Aerodynamic Analysis Using CFD

Performed high-speed aerodynamic simulation and optimization of a 2D supersonic missile using ANSYS Fluent. The study evaluated drag and lift performance under varying attack angles while applying advanced structured meshing and turbulence modelling techniques.
